About Pending™

Pending™ explores the illusion of power in AI-driven governance, where corporate-controlled platforms dictate the rules of online existence.

As an operator recruited by United Systems (U.S), your job is to review user profiles and decide who is allowed to stay online.

Your decisions will shape the future of digital society—will you comply?
